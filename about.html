Who We Are

Built on trust,
driven by results

AN Build is the operational brand of Ashie Holdings and Projects U Ltd, a Ugandan company incorporated and committed to transforming the built environment across East Africa.

Founded over a decade ago, we have grown from a local construction firm into a diversified holding company with interests in property development, infrastructure, and long-term asset management.

Our work is guided by a simple belief: that great buildings and infrastructure create the conditions for communities to flourish. Every project we undertake is an investment in Uganda's future.
